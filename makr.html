UX and UI

Once the user is logged in they are prompted with the option to fill out the basic profile. They are able to decline and finish it later, but they won't be matched until they do.

The goal was to make any process that required the user to fill out the information to be as simple as possible. The photo upload section of the sign-up process was reduced to just one required photo instead of multiple photos.

The "Like" feature is different from others in that once both users like each other a 24-hour communication window opens up.
